Alfredo Garcia Revuelta was born on April 22, 1961 in Madrid, Spain.
He is an artist at heart, a citizen of the world and inveterate traveler.




"His work has the simplicity and depth of the proverbs. It is direct and clear without being superficial, minimal without being empty, with a sense of humor without diverting its meaning and intelligent without sinking into the comfort of his own intelligence.

Emotionally powerful and refreshingly free of irony not reminds us the basics, the essential stuff of life.

In a world crowded this memory is extremely welcome."


Holly Myers
Los Angeles Times.
